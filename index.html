I am

UX/UI designer, entrepreneur, Author, product design

An UX/UI designer and 'wanna-be' entrepreneur/Author, realised to the path of product design, So that I can bring to life the wonderland in my mind!

With design in my soul and Coding in my mind i am a quitisential concoction of Art and Technology who wants to make peoples lives better by making the "stuff" they use better.
A lot of the work that I have done so far, is powered through the most bizarre questions in my mind.... From what happens if we add a few protons to a helium atom can we create Vibranium and make a shield around earth,TO can it be that we are all living oblivious of the magic in the myths around us, after all maybe the titans are watching over us and clearing our memories?
I get lost in the details time and again, inspiration finds its way in and curiosity conquers the Perfectionistic maniac in me.
Though I never powered through all of my creations, to make the perfect end product. I am still proud to have quelled through my fears and insecurities to make SOMETHING!? from the Multiverse that lies in my brain.
